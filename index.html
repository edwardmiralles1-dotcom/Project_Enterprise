Exhibit 6

Recommended actions for management

1

Close the three remaining Medium-residual ISMS risks

DevOps DR replication, DR server capacity, and branch link redundancy are the last controls standing between the register and an all-Low residual position.

2

Re-baseline the five BCMS "Critical" ratings

Confirm them against the numeric Consequence × Likelihood scale so BCMS and ISMS priorities are directly comparable in future combined reports.

3

Review key-person / succession risk

System Administrator and Network Administrator together carry 44% of all logged BCMS + ISMS risk — both a single point of accountability and a key-person dependency for treatment.

4

Prioritize "Systems, Applications and Databases" for the next control-investment cycle

This category alone accounts for 51% of ISMS risk volume — the highest-leverage area for further control spend.

5

Rate the one unrated BCMS scenario

One BCMS risk has no priority rating in the source register — obtain a rating before the next reporting cycle so totals are complete.
